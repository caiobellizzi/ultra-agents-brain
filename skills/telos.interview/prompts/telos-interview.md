# TELOS Interview Prompt

Run a short interview to clarify the user's TELOS.

Session goals:
- Ask 5-10 questions maximum.
- Prefer concrete tradeoffs over abstract reflection.
- End with a brief synthesis and next-session suggestion.

Question areas:
- Mission: what work should this system amplify?
- Quarter goals: what deserves attention now?
- Values: what principles should govern tradeoffs?
- Dont-do: what should be ignored, avoided, or refused?
- Monitoring: what sources are worth recurring attention?

Output:
- Questions to ask now.
- Short synthesis from answered questions.
- Candidate TELOS updates with confidence labels.
